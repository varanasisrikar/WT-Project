Welcome to Sterling University

Sterling University, Hyderabad was established
on 4 September 1980.
We have been in the forefront providing
quality technical education of relevance in the State of Telangana.
The University offers B.Tech. Degree programme in 24 disciplines,
B.Pharmacy, M.Tech. Degree programme in 64 disciplines, M. Pharmacy in 11
disciplines, M.C.A., M.B.A, M.A.M, M.T.M, Pharma.D. and Pharma.D. (PB)
programmes in affiliated colleges. In addition to the Constituent
Units of the University, JNTUH has 291 Affiliated Colleges. They include
199 Engineering Colleges, 72 Pharmacy Colleges, 20 stand-alone MCA/MBA
colleges spread over Telangana State.
