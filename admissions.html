Admission Procedure

B Tech Programs

The Eligibility Criteria for Admission to B.Tech Program:

Admissions to the B.Tech Program are made along with the other Engineering colleges in the state through a common entrance test (EAMCET) conducted by the Govt. of Telangana State.

Admission

  1. 70 % of the seats are allotted based on the merit in the EAMCET.
  2. 30 % of the seats are earmarked for Management/NRI candidates.
  3. In addition to the above, Diploma holders are admitted in second year of B. Tech to the extent of 20% of intake based on the merit in the ECET, under lateral entry scheme.
